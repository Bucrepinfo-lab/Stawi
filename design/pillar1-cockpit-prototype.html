Phone-first group cockpit

A self-contained, working prototype — no build, no libraries. It shows the flow you asked for: you sign in with a phone number (no email), and every group whose roster carries that number appears automatically, with the right role. Officials can capture the charter, minute a meeting, and let Stawi paraphrase it into a bank-ready document plus a month-end statement.
