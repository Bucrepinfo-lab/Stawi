# Stawi — Brand & Identity

**Stawi** is Swahili for *thrive / prosper*. The name carries the whole promise:
money that grows, groups that endure, businesses that scale — from the first
shilling saved to a listed institution.

**Tagline:** *Save · Grow · Thrive.*

---

## Vision

> A future where every saver, group and business can thrive on their own terms:
> where the first coin put into a savings circle and the books of a growing
> enterprise sit on the same trusted rails, and where formal finance is open to
> everyone — not just the already-banked.

## Mission

> To give every savings group, entrepreneur and enterprise **one trusted
> platform to save, borrow, trade and grow** — adapted to each community's
> currency, language, tax and registration rules — with the records, guard-rails
> and credit that turn informal effort into formal, fundable institutions. No one
> has to start small to be taken seriously, and no one is left behind by
> language, literacy or a cheap phone.

## Core values

1. **Trust** — Tamper-proof, audit-ready records by default. What Stawi shows a
   member, a bank or a regulator is the same truth.
2. **Inclusion** — Phone-first, multilingual (English, Kiswahili, Français,
   Español, Português, العربية — and growing), works offline on an entry-level
   device. Anyone can join **any pillar directly** — no gatekeeping, no need to
   graduate from step one.
3. **Discipline** — Built-in guard-rails against the well-researched ways savings
   groups and small businesses fail.
4. **Growth** — A ladder, not a ceiling: table banking → business → formal books
   → savings & credit → SACCO → bank. Stawi grows with you.
5. **Simplicity** — Self-directing. The app always answers "what do I do next?"
   so power comes without complexity.

---

## The four pillars (each joinable on its own)

| Pillar | What it is | Who joins directly |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1 · Records & Table Banking** | Group charter, minutes, month-end statements | Chamas, table-banking groups |
| **2 · Business Matching** | Turn capital into vetted ventures & supplier links | Investors, groups, entrepreneurs |
| **3 · Accounting & Compliance** | Pre-packed POS, books, tax, inventory, automation | Any business — SMEs to established firms |
| **4 · Savings & Credit (SACCO+)** | Digital savings, tiered credit, graduation | Savers, SACCOs, institutions |

A member interested in Pillars 2, 3 or 4 **subscribes directly** — Pillar 1 is a
fast-track for groups, not a prerequisite. This opens Stawi to everyone from a
single trader to an established enterprise.
